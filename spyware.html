Spyware

What is spyware?
-Spyware is a type of malware that tries to stay hidden while secretly recording information and tracking your online activities, both on computers and mobile devices. You can monitor and copy everything you write, upload, download, and store. Some strains of spyware are also capable of activating cameras and microphones to see and hear you without you noticing.

How does spyware operate?
- Spyware runs silently in the background and collects information or monitors its activity to carry out malicious actions that affect the computer and its use. This includes recording screenshots, keystrokes, authentication credentials, personal email addresses, online form data, internet usage information, and other personal data, such as credit card numbers.

Characteristics
-It has the ability to self-install in the system, which has been affected.
-It runs every time the computer is turned on and keeps running continuously.
-Cannot be replicated to other different computers.
