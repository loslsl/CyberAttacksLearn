General information

How does CyberAttack learn start?

We were in class learning stuff about how groups of hackers used ransomware as a way to attack our country, when we spoke to other people who don't really know what ransomware is or means, we understood that our country has a poorly knowledge when it comes to technology.
We decided to make a change by creating a website in which you can learn the main types of cyber attacks and get to know the world of cybersecutity a little bit more.

Objectives

  • Guide people about what cyber attacks are and the danger they represent for users.
  • Inform using media and virtual spaces.
  • Encourage the learning of topics related to computer science.
  • Recognize important concepts such as operation and characteristics of various malicious software in order to acquire greater knowledge about them.
